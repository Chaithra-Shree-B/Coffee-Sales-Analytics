☕ Coffee Shop Sales Analytics Dashboard

📊 Overview
This project presents an interactive Coffee Shop Sales Analytics Dashboard built using Tableau. It provides deep insights into sales performance, product trends, pricing behavior, and category contribution to help drive smarter business decisions.

🚀 Key Insights
- Top Performers: Barista Espresso and Coffee category drive the highest revenue
- Sales Growth: Consistent upward trend with strong performance in later months
- Customer Behavior: Certain high-priced items still achieve strong demand
- Category Contribution: Coffee dominates overall sales share (~38%)
- Product Strategy: Mix of high-volume and high-margin products identified

📌 Dashboard Features
- 📈 Sales Trend Over Time – Monthly performance tracking
- ☕ Sales by Coffee Type – Product-level comparison
- 🎯 Price vs Quantity Analysis – Customer buying patterns
- 🥧 Category Sales Share – Contribution of each product category
- 📊 Quantity vs Sales – Volume vs revenue insights

🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Tableau Public – Data visualization
- Data Analysis – Aggregations, trend analysis, KPI tracking

This dashboard helps stakeholders:
- Optimize pricing strategies
- Identify best-selling products
- Improve inventory planning
- Understand customer demand patterns
- Track business growth over time
